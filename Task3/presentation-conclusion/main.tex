\begin{frame}
\frametitle{Annotation Biases}
\begin{itemize}
\item Most files only have a single annotator $\rightarrow$ Lack of verification
\item Demographics and cultural bias
\item Definition of a ``typical household''?
\item Habits, routines and housing conditions affect the interpretation of scenarios.
\item The implication of a scenario just by a series of audio queues can lead to false assumptions.
\item Example: Clicking sound after opening a door.
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}
\frametitle{Broader impact of public available data}
\begin{itemize}
\item Development of new applications for accessibility, safety and security
\item Highest accident rate in households.
\item Detect accidents and take action immediately
\item Detect hazards like gas leaks.
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}
\frametitle{Broader impact of public available data}
\begin{itemize}
\item Concern for normalization of surveillance.
\item Enforcement of these systems as a security standard is not acceptable by everyone.
\item Promote ethical use by enforcing a license.
\item Specify restrictions and use conditions.
\item Ensure anonymity so that no personal information is leaked.
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
